Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Google Drive Integration

The sim8085 Simulator ("the App") requests access to your Google Drive to save assembly files created within the App. We request only the restricted drive.file scope, meaning the App can only see, edit, and create files that it has specifically created itself. We do not have access to any other files in your Google Drive.

2. Data Storage

All data processing and simulation occurs entirely locally in your web browser. Authentication tokens for Google Drive are handled directly by your browser and Google's servers. We do not transmit, collect, or store your authentication tokens, email address, or any of your files on our servers.

3. AI Assistant (Anthropic API)

If you use the AI Assistant feature, your Anthropic API key is stored securely in your browser's Local Storage. It is transmitted directly to Anthropic's API when you send a prompt. We do not proxy, intercept, or log your API key or conversation data.

4. Analytics

We use basic Google Analytics to understand generic web traffic. This does not track personal identifiable information or your code.
